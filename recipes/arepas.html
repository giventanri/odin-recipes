Arepas

Description

Food made of ground maize dough, popular in Latin America with many regional varities. Delicious by itself and when paired.

Ingredients

  • 1 c water
  • 1 c corn meal
  • 1 c shredded mozzarella
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Mix water, corn meal, mozzarella, butter, and salt
  2. Knead until soft consistency
  3. Form dough into balls and flatten
  4. Cut dough into circles
  5. Coat griddle with cooking spray
  6. Grill until golden brown
