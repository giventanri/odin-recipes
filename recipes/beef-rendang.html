Beef Rendang

Description

One of Indonesia's most famous dishes: beef slow-cooked in coconut milk and spices.

Ingredients

Spice paste:

  • 7 long chiles
  • 6 shallots
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 ginger
  • 1 galangal
  • 1 turmeric
  • 2 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp cumin

Beef:

  • 2 1/4 lb beef
  • 3 1/3 c coconut milk
  • 2 lemongrass stalks
  • 5 lime leaves
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp sea salt

Steps

  1. Combine spice into food processor
  2. Trim and cut beef into chunks
  3. Add spice paste and beef into coconut milk
  4. Boil then simmer for 2 hours; stir to avoid sticking
  5. After 2 hours, discard oil and lemongrass
  6. Reduce until thickened and browned
